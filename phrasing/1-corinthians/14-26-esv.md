# 1 Corinthians 14:26 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** When the church gathers, every member contributes for edification — variety of gifts, one aim: build up.

## Text

> [26] What then, brothers? When you come together, each one has a hymn, a lesson, a revelation, a tongue, or an interpretation. Let all things be done for building up.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[26] What then, brothers?
    When you come together,
        each one has a hymn,
        a lesson,
        a revelation,
        a tongue,
            or an interpretation.
    Let all things be done
        for building up.
```

## Notes

- **26** — Corporate gathering assumes broad participation, not passive attendance.
- Parallel list of contributions shows diversity; governing principle is single — edification.
- Useful for teaching what healthy group gatherings aim at: mutual upbuilding, not performance.
