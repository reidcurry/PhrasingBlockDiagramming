# Hebrews 10:23–25 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Why gather — hold fast to hope, stir one another up, and do not neglect meeting together.

## Text

> [23] Let us hold fast the confession of our hope without wavering, for he who promised is faithful. [24] And let us consider how to stir up one another to love and good works, [25] not neglecting to meet together, as is the habit of some, but encouraging one another, and all the more as you see the Day drawing near.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[23] Let us hold fast the confession of our hope without wavering,
        for he who promised is faithful.
[24] And let us consider
        how to stir up one another
            to love and good works,
[25] not neglecting to meet together,
        as is the habit of some,
    but encouraging one another,
        and all the more
            as you see the Day drawing near.
```

## Notes

- **23** — Community perseverance rests on God's faithfulness, not our wavering.
- **24** — Active consideration: how to provoke love and good works in one another.
- **25** — Meeting together is the means of encouragement; neglect is a drift the author confronts.
