# Romans 12:9–13 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Marks of genuine love in community — real affection, honor, zeal, endurance, and hospitality.

## Text

> [9] Let love be genuine. Abhor what is evil; hold fast to what is good. [10] Love one another with brotherly affection. Outdo one another in showing honor. [11] Do not be slothful in zeal, be fervent in spirit, serve the Lord. [12] Rejoice in hope, be patient in tribulation, be constant in prayer. [13] Contribute to the needs of the saints and seek to show hospitality.

## Notes

- **9–10** — Genuine love is discriminating (evil/good) and competitive in honor, not status.
- **11–12** — Inner fervor and outward endurance mark community under pressure.
- **13** — Practical generosity and opened homes embody the love commanded above.
