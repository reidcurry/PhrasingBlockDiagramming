# Proverbs 27:17 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Mutual sharpening — God designed people to refine one another through close, honest relationship.

## Text

> [17] Iron sharpens iron, and one man sharpens another.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[17] Iron sharpens iron,
        and one man sharpens another.
```

## Notes

- **17** — Parallel structure: material sharpening pictures relational growth through contact and friction.
- Short proverb suited for introducing the need for regular, face-to-face community.
