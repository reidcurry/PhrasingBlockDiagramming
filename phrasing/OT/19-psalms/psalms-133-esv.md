# Psalm 133 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Unity is good and pleasant — brothers dwelling together receive blessing pictured in priestly anointing and Mount Hermon's dew.

## Text

> [1] Behold, how good and pleasant it is when brothers dwell in unity! [2] It is like the precious oil on the head, running down on the beard, on the beard of Aaron, running down on the collar of his robes! [3] It is like the dew of Hermon, which falls on the mountains of Zion! For there the LORD has commanded the blessing, life forevermore.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[1] Behold, how good and pleasant it is
        when brothers dwell in unity!
[2] It is like the precious oil on the head,
        running down on the beard,
            on the beard of Aaron,
                running down on the collar of his robes!
[3] It is like the dew of Hermon,
        which falls on the mountains of Zion!
    For there the LORD has commanded the blessing,
        life forevermore.
```

## Notes

- **1** — Unity among brothers is celebrated, not merely tolerated.
- **2** — Oil imagery ties unity to consecration and priestly blessing overflowing downward.
- **3** — Dew from Hermon to Zion pictures abundant life where God commands eternal blessing.
