# Hebrews 13:17 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Dever's membership text — members submit to leaders who watch over souls and give account to God; meaningful membership makes pastoral care possible.

## Text

> [17] Obey your leaders and submit to them, for they are keeping watch over your souls, as those who will have to give an account. Let them do this with joy and not with groaning, for that would be of no advantage to you.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[17] Obey your leaders
        and submit to them,
    for they are keeping watch over your souls,
        as those who will have to give an account.
    Let them do this with joy
        and not with groaning,
    for that would be of no advantage to you.
```

## Notes

- **17** — Submission is tied to soul-care and future accountability, not mere organizational hierarchy.
- **17** — Members who make shepherding burdensome harm themselves as well as their leaders.
