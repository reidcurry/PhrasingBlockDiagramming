# Ephesians 4:1–3 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Dever's unity text — community groups must serve church-wide unity, not fragment the body; maintain the Spirit's bond in humility and patience.

## Text

> [1] I therefore, a prisoner for the Lord, urge you to walk in a manner worthy of the calling to which you have been called, [2] with all humility and gentleness, with patience, bearing with one another in love, [3] eager to maintain the unity of the Spirit in the bond of peace.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[1] I therefore, a prisoner for the Lord,
    urge you to walk
        in a manner worthy of the calling to which you have been called,
[2] with all humility and gentleness,
        with patience,
        bearing with one another in love,
[3] eager to maintain the unity of the Spirit
        in the bond of peace.
```

## Notes

- **1** — Worthy walking flows from the gospel calling, not from program success.
- **2** — Virtues that make diverse community possible are relational, not organizational.
- **3** — Unity is active maintenance — Dever warns groups can either foster or fracture it.
