# John 13:34–35 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Christ's new command — love one another as he loved us; this love marks disciples before the watching world.

## Text

> [34] A new commandment I give to you, that you love one another: just as I have loved you, you also are to love one another. [35] By this all people will know that you are my disciples, if you have love for one another.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[34] A new commandment I give to you,
        that you love one another:
    just as I have loved you,
        you also are to love one another.
[35] By this all people will know
        that you are my disciples,
        if you have love for one another.
```

## Notes

- **34** — The standard is Christ's own love, not mere natural affection.
- **35** — Community love is public witness; outsiders recognize disciples by it.
