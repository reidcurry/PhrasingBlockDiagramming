# Ephesians 2:19–22 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** No longer outsiders — fellow citizens and household members built together into God's dwelling by the Spirit.

## Text

> [19] So then you are no longer strangers and aliens, but you are fellow citizens with the saints and members of the household of God, [20] built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Christ Jesus himself being the cornerstone, [21] in whom the whole structure, being joined together, grows into a holy temple in the Lord. [22] In him you also are being built together into a dwelling place for God by the Spirit.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[19] So then you are no longer strangers and aliens,
        but you are fellow citizens with the saints
            and members of the household of God,
[20] built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets,
        Christ Jesus himself being the cornerstone,
[21] in whom the whole structure,
        being joined together,
        grows into a holy temple in the Lord.
[22] In him you also are being built together
        into a dwelling place for God
            by the Spirit.
```

## Notes

- **19** — Identity shift: from alien to citizen and family member.
- **20–21** — Christ-centered architecture: apostles/prophets foundation, Christ cornerstone, growing temple.
- **22** — Present tense "being built together" — community formation is ongoing work of the Spirit.
