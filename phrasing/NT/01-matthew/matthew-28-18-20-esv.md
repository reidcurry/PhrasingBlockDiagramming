# Matthew 28:18–20 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Dever's discipling charter — every Christian helps others follow Jesus through baptism, teaching, and obedience in the local church.

## Text

> [18] And Jesus came and said to them, "All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. [19] Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, [20] teaching them to observe all that I have commanded you. And behold, I am with you always, to the end of the age."

## Notes

- **18** — Christ's universal authority grounds the commission.
- **19–20** — Discipling is corporate and concrete: go, baptize into church identity, teach obedience.
- **20** — The promise of presence sustains lifelong, relational disciple-making.
