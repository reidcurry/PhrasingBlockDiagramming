# Galatians 3:28 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Dever's gospel-unity text — compelling community crosses natural divisions; groups should mirror the whole church, not affinity cliques.

## Text

> [28] There is neither Jew nor Greek, there is neither slave nor free, there is no male and female, for you are all one in Christ Jesus.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[28] There is neither Jew nor Greek,
        there is neither slave nor free,
        there is no male and female,
    for you are all one in Christ Jesus.
```

## Notes

- **28** — Three pairs of dividing walls — ethnic, social, gender — overcome in Christ.
- **28** — Gospel-revealing community unites people who would not naturally cluster together.
