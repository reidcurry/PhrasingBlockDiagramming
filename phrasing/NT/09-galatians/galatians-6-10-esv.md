# Galatians 6:10 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Dever's household-of-faith text — general good to all, but prioritized care for church members; groups serve those bound by covenant.

## Text

> [10] So then, as we have opportunity, let us do good to everyone, and especially to those who are of the household of faith.

## Whole-passage flow

```
[10] So then,
    as we have opportunity,
    let us do good to everyone,
        and especially to those who are of the household of faith.
```

## Notes

- **10** — Christian love is universal in scope but intensified toward the local church family.
- **10** — Pairs with 6:1–2: restoration and burden-bearing are how "especially" looks in practice.
