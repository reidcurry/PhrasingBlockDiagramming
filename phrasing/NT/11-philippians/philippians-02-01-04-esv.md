# Philippians 2:1–4 (ESV)

**Sermon angle:** Unified mind in community — shared Christ-experience leads to humility and others-first living.

## Text

> [1] So if there is any encouragement in Christ, any comfort from love, any participation in the Spirit, any affection and sympathy, [2] complete my joy by being of the same mind, having the same love, being in full accord and of one mind. [3] Do nothing from selfish ambition or conceit, but in humility count others more significant than yourselves. [4] Let each of you look not only to his own interests, but also to the interests of others.

## Notes

- **1–2** — Four "if any" clauses ground unity in shared life in Christ, Spirit, and affection.
- **3** — Unity requires rejecting rivalry and adopting others-oriented humility.
- **4** — Community groups train the habit of attending to others' interests, not only one's own.
